CHIMERA

PRAYER

Accompanying poem for River Idol

rivers join, wrestle smoke and silt intertwine timeless Father of Waters carrying life, spirit given new names with no meanings from reverence to disregard now carrying death, devils

oh, great river at this heart, one seat a token of worship memory for the spirits before and behind

built around the prodigal mustang, caballo, soba lost and returned but this time with bloodlust wild spirit, bound saddled bucking against restraints

the eagle's sight, wazika xowe sacred savenger stolen, divorced revered still but at the altar of consumption a vision of death broken from its cycles of rebirth

life-source buffalo, yunush, miisiikwaha cornerstone shattered castles of skulls on the plains rivers of blood beast, then human only a vestige remains

rattlesnake, manetoa, weh-ts'ah lashing, fanged icon of medicine and healing poisoned by cries of devil in a garden now sealed fallen from grace

oh, great river vessel and watcher and truthsayer be enshrined as our memory join, wrestle intertwine
